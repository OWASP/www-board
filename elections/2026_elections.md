### Call For Candidates

Paid Individual Members who have been in good standing for more than 12 months can stand for the Board. To apply, please search your email for the title "in:all OWASP 2026 Global Board Election Kickoff", which includes all details.

The full eligibility requirements for candidates can be found in the OWASP Bylaws, Sections 3.12 and 4.3 and the Director's Policy. The OWASP Bylaws can be found at [https://policy.owasp.org/legal/bylaws](https://policy.owasp.org/legal/bylaws) and the Director's Policy can be found at [https://policy.owasp.org/operational/director](https://policy.owasp.org/operational/director). 

Once elected, successful candidates will serve a two year term on the OWASP Board of Directors, beginning January 1, 2027 and ending December 31, 2029. They are required to complete a Director's Commitment Agreement, which includes a commitment to maintain good standing as a paid member of OWASP throughout their term, complete a conflict of interest disclosure, potentially undergo a background check (particularly if Chair and or Treasurer), and abide by the OWASP Bylaws, Code of Conduct, Anti Trust and Director's Policy. The Director's Commitment Agreement can be found at [https://policy.owasp.org/legal/directors-committment-agreement](https://policy.owasp.org/legal/directors-committment-agreement).

Please familiarize yourself with these policies and agreements before submitting your nomination. If you have any questions, please contact the [Executive Director](mailto:executive.director@owasp.com).

If you are unable to meet the eligibility requirements, or abide by these policies and agreements, you are not eligible to serve on the OWASP Board of Directors.

**Directors are solely responsible for maintaining good standing as a paid member throughout their term, or a vote of confidence will be held by the Board. This has happened several times in the past. We strongly urge Director-elects to take out sufficient membership to cover their entire term to avoid issues with standing. Adding a two year membership to your current membership or changing to a Lifetime membership is the ideal way to avoid this issue entirely.**

### Board Election Results - to be announced on November 6 2026

Once voting has finished, the following business day, the OWASP Foundation will confirm the eligibility of the potentially elected candidates for open vacancies, and ask the candidates if they accept their win. The OWASP Foundation gives candidates a few days to respond. If there is no response by 11:59 pm US Eastern time on November 5 2026, the next candidate(s) will be selected and asked if they wish to serve until all vacancies are filled. The OWASP Foundation will announce the results of the election on November 6 2026, with all filled vacancies and the names of the elected candidates. The results will be published on the OWASP website and sent to all members via email.
